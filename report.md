# Final Report on Travel Insurance Policy Review 

## Introduction
In light of your dissatisfaction with your current travel insurance policy, GREAT TravelCare, this report aims to provide a comprehensive analysis to inform your decision regarding potential alternatives. The analysis encompasses an examination of the existing policy's coverage, a guide to the claims process, a cost comparison with similar products, and financial recommendations tailored to optimize your insurance coverage.

## Insurance Coverage Analysis

### Current Policy Overview: GREAT TravelCare (Tier: Platinum)
- **Coverage for Pre-existing Conditions:** Up to S$200,000 for medical expenses and S$15,000 for trip cancellations resulting from pre-existing conditions. 
- **General Protection:** Coverage includes cancellation, postponement, or shortening of the trip for any reason, starting from the time the policy is purchased, provided it is within 30 days of travel booking.
- **Travel Delays:** Cash payout for delays starting from four hours.
- **Adventure and Leisure Activities:** Coverage includes participation in leisure adventurous activities, subject to policy terms.

### Exclusions and Limitations:
- **Exclusions:** Notably, there may be limitations regarding the types of adventure sports covered and specific restrictions on the nature of pre-existing conditions that may not be fully covered. 
- **Coverage Gaps:** The current policy's exclusion of certain high-risk activities or medical conditions may necessitate reviewing additional options tailored to your travel needs.

## Claims Process Guide

### Step-by-Step Claims Procedure for Current Policy:
1. **Notification:** Inform GREAT Eastern via phone or email about the impending claim.
2. **Complete Claim Form:** Access and fill out the claim form on their website.
3. **Gather Documentation:** Prepare necessary documents, including proof of travel, medical receipts, and other supporting documents.
4. **Submission:** Submit the claim form along with all documentation to the claims department as outlined in your policy.
5. **Follow-Up:** Contact the insurer within a week if there hasn’t been acknowledgment of receipt.

### Documentation Requirements and Timelines:
- A claim must be submitted within 30 days from the date of incident (e.g., trip cancellation, medical emergency).
- Required documents typically include travel itinerary, receipts, medical reports, and any related correspondence.

## Cost Analysis

### Current Premiums and Projections:
You are currently paying S$800 annually for your existing GREAT TravelCare policy. Considering alternatives, the projected costs for similar policies range from S$780 to S$900, depending on the coverage level desired.

### Out-of-Pocket Cost Projections:
- In the event of a medical claim, out-of-pocket costs may include any excess amounts beyond the coverage limits, which should be accounted for when choosing a new policy.

## Financial Recommendations

1. **Conduct a Detailed Needs Assessment:** Evaluate travel habits and activity levels to determine the types of activities likely to be undertaken and ensure that selected insurance covers them appropriately.
   
2. **Alternatives for Specific Coverage:** Consider plans specific to adventure sports if these activities are part of your travel plans.

3. **Look for Discounts:** Investigate if policy discount programs are available, especially for bundling multiple types of insurance or rewards for low claims history.

4. **Track Policy Terms:** When comparing policies, attention should be paid to the specific terms regarding pre-existing conditions, as different insurance providers have varying interpretations and limitations that can affect claims.

## Conclusion
The review of your current travel insurance, GREAT TravelCare, identifies potential shortcomings in coverage that could be improved upon by switching to alternative policies. The analysis presented provides a framework for understanding different policy options, analyzing costs, and navigating claims processes, all critical for optimizing your insurance coverage during travel.

By pursuing the recommendations outlined and engaging in thorough comparative analysis, you can achieve a travel insurance policy tailored to your needs, enhancing peace of mind while travelling. It may also be prudent to engage with a professional insurance advisor who can assist in further clarifying these options and navigating potential upgrades.
