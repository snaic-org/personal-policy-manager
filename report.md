[Conclusion](#conclusion)\n\n## Introduction\n\nThe GREAT SupremeHealth policy is a health insurance plan offered by Great Eastern. This report aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the policy's coverage for cancer treatment, including its benefits, limits, and comparisons with other policies.\n\n## Policy Overview\n\nThe GREAT SupremeHealth policy covers various medical expenses, including cancer treatment. The policy has different plan types, each with its own set of benefits and limits. The plan type, hospital/ward class entitlement, expense item, and benefit limit are all important factors in determining the level of coverage.\n\n## Cancer Treatment Benefits\n\nThe GREAT SupremeHealth policy covers cancer treatment, including cancer drug treatment, up to 120 days before outpatient treatment. The policy also provides coverage of up to 95% of hospitalization costs, with a deductible incurred under the Life Assured's corresponding GREAT SupremeHealth plan.\n\nFor outpatient cancer drug treatment on the Cancer Drug List, the policy has a special benefit limit of 15x (MediShield Life's limit for one primary cancer) for Life Assured receiving treatment for one primary cancer. Radiation therapy treatment duration varies by individual and can depend on factors such as location and stage of disease, type of cancer, age, and general health.\n\n## Comparisons with Other Policies\n\nOther health insurance policies, such as AIA HealthShield Gold Max and PRUShield Premier, offer similar coverage for cancer treatment. AIA HealthShield Gold Max offers 4 plan types with unlimited lifetime claims and various add-ons such as Cancer Care Booster and Emergency and Outpatient Care Booster. PRUShield Premier provides coverage of up to S$10,000 per month for multiple primary cancers.\n\n## Research Learnings\n\nResearch learnings provide additional information on cancer treatment coverage and limits for various health insurance policies. These learnings highlight the importance of carefully reviewing policy details to ensure adequate coverage for cancer treatment.\n\n## Conclusion\n\nIn conclusion, the GREAT SupremeHealth policy offers comprehensive coverage for cancer treatment, including cancer drug treatment and radiation therapy. While other policies, such as AIA HealthShield Gold Max and PRUShield Premier, offer similar coverage, it is essential to carefully review policy details to ensure adequate coverage for cancer treatment.\n\nBy understanding the benefits, limits, and comparisons with other policies, individuals can make informed decisions when selecting a health insurance policy that meets their needs.
